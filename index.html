Slow cooker marry me chicken sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 sandwich buns
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts in a slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the chicken broth, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it with two forks.
  6. Return the shredded chicken to the slow cooker and stir to combine with the sauce.
  7. Serve the chicken on sandwich buns and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
